Activities at Pacific Trails

Fishing

Pacific Trails Resort is next to a beautiful lake, where we offer fishing rods for those looking to catch cods.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week.

Golfing

A mile East where the Yurts are, you'll find our golf course with a series of 9 holes each including tee, fairway, and putting green.
